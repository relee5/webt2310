"When are you going to open a bakery?" is the half-joking question I have been asked any time I've made dessert for a crowd since I really started baking in high school. I've always laughed it off, never thinking it could be a reality for me. Those early mornings baking bread, the massive overhead needed for a professional kitchen, the idea of introverted little me having to interview and hire and maybe even fire people all sounded overwhelming and out of reach.

Meeting my entpreneurial-spirited (and sweet-toothed) husband changed everything for me. He convinced me that I had not only the drive but the talent to do this. I started out small with custom orders in a rented kitchen space, and now here we are with a professional bakery and storefront!

I specialize in making rustic breads and desserts. Check out our menu to see what we offer in our storefront daily. For custom orders, I offer fully decorated cakes, cupcakes, cookies, and more. To learn more about custom items, please get in touch with us so we can talk more about what you're looking for!

My goal for Becca's Bakery is to bring people together in a comfy space with delicious bread, decadent pastries, and amazing coffee. This is a place to retreat from the busy-ness of the world and refresh your soul (and your stomach). Come in and get to know us!
